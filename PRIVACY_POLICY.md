## 3. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

**a. Information you provide to us:**

- Account details: name, email address, phone number, password (stored only in encrypted form by our authentication provider), profile photo, and assigned role.
- Business data you enter: customer records, addresses, phone numbers, notes, job records, scheduling information, signatures, and invoice details.
- Communications you initiate through the App (for example, when you contact support).

**b. Information collected automatically:**

- Device information: device model, operating system and version, device language, time zone, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and crash diagnostics.
- Usage information: screens visited, features used, in-app actions, and approximate session duration.
- Log information: IP address, access times, and similar technical details.

**c. Information collected with your permission:**

- Precise location: only when you grant location permission, used to display your map view, route to job sites, and timestamp arrivals.
- Camera and photo library: only when you grant the relevant permission, used to capture and attach before/after job photos. The App does **not** scan or upload your full camera roll.
- Calendar access: only when you grant permission, used to add job entries to your device calendar.
- Contacts: only when you choose to import a specific contact to create a customer record. The App does **not** upload your contacts to our servers.
- Push notification token: only after you grant notification permission.

**d. Information from third parties:**

- Authentication providers and analytics services may share basic account or usage details with us as described in their own privacy policies.

We do **not** knowingly collect Social Security numbers, government-issued ID numbers, biometric identifiers, or precise health or financial account information from end users.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the App.
- Authenticate users and protect against unauthorized access.
- Provide the core scheduling, customer-management, mapping, and job-tracking features.
- Send transactional and operational communications (e.g., job reminders, account notices).
- Send push notifications you have opted in to receive.
- Analyze usage to diagnose problems and improve the App.
- Display, measure, and limit advertising (where applicable).
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service and EULA.
- Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, security incidents, and unlawful activity.

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing

Where required by applicable law, we process personal information on the following legal bases: (i) performance of a contract with you; (ii) compliance with legal obligations; (iii) your consent (which you may withdraw at any time); and (iv) our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the App in a manner that does not override your rights.

## 6. How We Share Information

We do **not** sell personal information for monetary consideration. We may share information only as follows:

- **Service providers** acting on our behalf under written agreements (cloud hosting, authentication, database, file storage, analytics, error monitoring, push delivery, mapping, and — where applicable — payment processing).
- **Within your organization:** Account holders for the same B.CLEAN-licensed business may see each other's job, schedule, customer, and availability data as required for the App to function.
- **Compliance and protection:** When required by law, subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or when we believe disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect our rights, the rights of users, or public safety.
- **Business transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of all or part of our assets, subject to standard confidentiality protections.
- **With your consent:** For any other purpose disclosed to you at the time of collection.

The current third-party services that process information for the App include:

- **Google / Firebase** — authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, and analytics.
- **Stripe** (via secure server-side functions, only if you use payment features) — payment processing. We do **not** store full card numbers.
- **Google Maps Platform** — maps, geocoding, and routing.
- **Google AdMob** — advertising (only when enabled in your build).
- **Expo Application Services** — push notification delivery and over-the-air updates.

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy and security practices.

## 7. Advertising and Analytics

If advertising is enabled in your build of the App:

- We default to **non-personalized ads** so we do not need to track you across other apps and websites.
- You may opt out of personalized advertising at any time:
  - **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.
  - **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization".
- AdMob may use device identifiers, IP address, and similar technologies to deliver ads and frequency-cap them.

We use lightweight analytics to understand which features are used. Aggregated and anonymized analytics may be retained indefinitely.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active, as needed to provide the App, and as required by applicable law. Business records you create (customers, jobs, invoices) are retained for the life of your subscription and for a reasonable period thereafter for backup, legal-defense, audit, and tax purposes. You may request earlier deletion as described below.

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information, including:

- TLS/HTTPS encryption in transit.
- Server-side encryption at rest with our cloud providers.
- Role-based access controls (Admin, Salesperson, Technician).
- Firestore and Cloud Storage security rules enforcing data access boundaries on every request.
- Authenticated API access only.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adults in a business setting and is **not** directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions where that higher age applies). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us at business@bclean.app and we will delete it.

## 11. Your Privacy Rights

The following rights apply where granted by law. We honor verifiable requests from any U.S. resident at the level of protection their state requires, regardless of location.

**a. Universal rights you have under this Policy (all U.S. states and territories):**

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction** — request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal-retention exceptions).
- **Portability** — request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
- **Withdraw consent** — withdraw consent for permission-based processing (location, camera, contacts, calendar, notifications) at any time through your device settings.
- **Opt out of advertising personalization** — at any time through device settings.

**b. State-specific rights:**

- **California (CCPA / CPRA):** You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and (if applicable) sell or share; the right to delete; the right to correct; the right to limit use of "sensitive personal information"; the right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (we do not sell or share for cross-context behavioral advertising); and the right to non-discrimination. Authorized agents may submit requests on your behalf with proof of authority.
- **Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA), Texas (TDPSA), Oregon (OCPA), Montana (MCDPA), Iowa (ICDPA), Tennessee (TIPA), Indiana (INCDPA), Delaware (DPDPA), New Hampshire (NHPA), New Jersey (NJDPA), Nebraska (NEDPA), Maryland (MODPA), Minnesota (MCDPA), Rhode Island (RIDPA), Kentucky (KCDPA), and any other comprehensive state privacy law:** You have the right to confirm processing, access, correct (where the law provides), delete, port your data, and opt out of sale, targeted advertising, and certain forms of profiling. We honor authenticated requests at the highest standard required by your state of residence.
- **Nevada (SB 220):** Nevada residents may opt out of any future sale of covered information. We do not currently sell covered information.
- **Illinois (BIPA), Washington (My Health My Data), and similar specialty laws:** We do not collect biometric identifiers or consumer health data through the App.

**c. How to exercise your rights:**

Email **business@bclean.app** with the subject line "**Privacy Request**" and a description of your request. We will verify your identity using the email associated with your account and respond within the time required by applicable law (typically 30 to 45 days, extendable as permitted). You will not be discriminated against for exercising any right granted by law.

**d. Right to appeal:**

If we decline a privacy request, you may appeal by replying to our response. We will respond to the appeal within 60 days, or as your state law requires. If the appeal is denied, you may contact your state attorney general.
